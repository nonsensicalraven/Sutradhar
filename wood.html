Wood, Metal, Terracotta, and Stone Traditions

Stone crafts include temple sculpture, jali latticework, and everyday utensils carved in marble, sandstone, and granite, especially from Rajasthan, Odisha, and Tamil Nadu. Lacquer crafts like Etikoppaka, Channapatna, and Kutch turn wood and gourds glossy for toys, bangles, and decor, while allied traditions—bidri inlay, bell-metal casting, terracotta, cane-bamboo, and wood carving—shape durable objects with simple, regional motifs.
