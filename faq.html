FolkArt Connect

Preserving Indian Heritage in the Digital Age

What is FolkArt Connect?

It is a digital platform to promote and preserve traditional Indian artforms like Warli, Pithora, and Madhubani.

How can artists showcase their artwork?

Artists can create profiles, upload images of their art, and share stories behind their creations.

Can buyers support artists?

Yes! Buyers can purchase art directly, commission custom work, or support artists via donations.

What artforms are promoted?

The artforms mainly promoted are categorised as painting and scroll traditions, textile, dyeing and printing arts, ritual floor and body arts, masks, puppetry and performing crafts, wood, metal, Terracotta and stone traditions, weaving and loom heritage. More detailed information is provided in the artforms section.

Can I contact artists directly?

Yes, each artist profile has contact details or a message option.

Can I sell my artwork on FolkArt Connect?

Yes, each artist profile has contact details or a message option.

Is there a membership fee for artists?

No, joining FolkArt Connect is free for artists. Some premium features may require a subscription in the future.

Are the artworks original and authentic?

All artists are verified and their work is original. We encourage traditional techniques and authentic folk styles.

Can I buy digital versions of the artwork?

Yes, digital downloads of certain artworks are available for purchase with permission from the artist.

How do I commission a custom artwork?

Buyers can directly contact the artist via the profile page to request a personalized piece.

Can I follow my favorite artists?

Yes, you can follow artists to get updates when they post new artwork or exhibitions.

Does FolkArt Connect organize offline events?

Occasionally, we collaborate with local exhibitions, workshops, and art festivals to promote folk art.

How is my payment secured?

All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways ensuring safe purchases.
