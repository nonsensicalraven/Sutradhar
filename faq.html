FolkArt Connect

Preserving Indian Heritage in the Digital Age

1. What is FolkArt Connect?

FolkArt Connect is an online platform dedicated to promoting and preserving traditional folk arts from across India. It connects artists, enthusiasts, and learners to celebrate the diversity of folk art.

2. Who can join FolkArt Connect?

Anyone interested in folk arts—artists, students, educators, or enthusiasts—can join the platform to explore, learn, and participate in the community.

3. Do I need to be an artist to participate?

No. You can join to learn about folk arts, participate in discussions, attend workshops, or simply enjoy exploring the various art forms.

4. How can I learn about different folk art forms on the platform?

FolkArt Connect provides detailed pages for each art form, including history, techniques, notable artists, photos, videos, and tutorials.

5. Can buyers support artists?

Not yet—but we are aiming to enable buyers to purchase artwork or commission custom work in the future.
